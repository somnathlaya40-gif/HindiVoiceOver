## 🎬 Professional Movie Voice Over App

**12 Professional Male Hindi Voices - Perfect for Movie Explanations!**

### 📥 Download & Install:

1. Download `HindiVoiceOver-v1.0.apk`
2. Open file on your Android phone
3. Tap "Install"
4. Done! Open app and start creating voice-overs

✅ 12 Professional Male Voices
✅ Pitch Control (-100 to +100)

✅ HD MP3 Export
